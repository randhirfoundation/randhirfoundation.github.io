About Us

Randhir Foundation was established with a vision to provide quality education to all segments of society. What started as a Online Teaching platform has now grown into a full-fledged educational foundation with plans to expand into school and college education.


Our mission is to make high-quality education accessible and affordable while maintaining the highest standards of teaching. We believe in nurturing not just academic excellence but also character and values.


Our future roadmap includes establishing a Coaching Center by 2028, a school by 2032, and a degree college by 2040, creating a complete educational ecosystem.
